2025 IOP Outstanding Reviewer Award

Our PI has been awarded the 2025 IOP Outstanding Reviewer Award twice this year for his contribution to journals published by IOP Publishing. In addition, first-year doctoral student Jincheng Yue also received this award in recognition of his sustained contributions as a reviewer for JPCM, which began with his first co-review of a manuscript with the PI.
